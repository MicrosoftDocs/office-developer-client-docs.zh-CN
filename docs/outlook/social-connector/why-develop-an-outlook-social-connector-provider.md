<span data-ttu-id="3b924-104">Outlook Social Connector (.osc) 提供程序允许 office 用户在 office 客户端应用程序中查看在专业或社交网络网站上应用的社交信息更新的聚合。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3b924-104">An Outlook Social Connector (OSC) provider allows Office users to view within an Office client application an aggregation of social information updates applied on a professional or social network site.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3b924-105">sharepoint Server、sharepoint Workspace、Lync 客户端和所有支持状态信息和联系人卡片支持 .osc 的 Office 客户端应用程序。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3b924-105">SharePoint Server, SharePoint Workspace, Lync client, and all Office client applications that support presence information and the Contact Card support the OSC.</span></span>
  
<span data-ttu-id="3b924-106">通过使用社交网络提供程序, .osc 会在联系人卡片或 Outlook 人员窗格中显示有关某人的其他信息, 如图1中所示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3b924-106">Using a social network provider, the OSC shows additional information about a person in the Contact Card or in the Outlook People Pane, as shown in Figure 1.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3b924-107">由于 .osc 是基于开放的提供程序模型构建的, 因此您可以很方便地构建提供程序与 .osc 配合使用, 以在 Office 中显示社交网络数据 (如朋友、个人资料、活动和关系信息)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3b924-107">Because the OSC is built on an open provider model, you can conveniently build providers to work with the OSC to display social network data (such as friends, profiles, activities, and relationship information) in Office.</span></span>
  
<span data-ttu-id="3b924-108">除了公共社交网络网站之外, 您还可以使用 .osc 提供程序扩展性为业务线应用程序或内部公司网站构建提供程序, 并将其服务集成到 Office 中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3b924-108">Apart from public social network sites, you can also use OSC provider extensibility to build providers for line-of-business applications or internal corporate web sites and to integrate their services into Office.</span></span>
