<span data-ttu-id="afa54-104">Outlook Social Connector (OSC) 提供程序允许 Office 用户查看 Office 客户端应用程序的社交信息更新汇总于 professional 或社交网络站点中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="afa54-104">An Outlook Social Connector (OSC) provider allows Office users to view within an Office client application an aggregation of social information updates applied on a professional or social network site.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="afa54-105">SharePoint Server、 SharePoint Workspace，Lync 客户端和所有 Office 客户端应用程序支持的状态信息和联系人卡片支持 OSC。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="afa54-105">SharePoint Server, SharePoint Workspace, Lync client, and all Office client applications that support presence information and the Contact Card support the OSC.</span></span>
  
<span data-ttu-id="afa54-106">使用社交网络提供程序，OSC 显示有关联系人的其他信息在联系人卡片或在 Outlook 的人员窗格中，如图 1 中所示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="afa54-106">Using a social network provider, the OSC shows additional information about a person in the Contact Card or in the Outlook People Pane, as shown in Figure 1.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="afa54-107">由于 OSC 基于一个打开的提供程序模型，因此您可以方便地生成 Office 中的提供程序以使用 OSC 显示社交网络数据 （如朋友、 配置文件、 活动和关系信息）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="afa54-107">Because the OSC is built on an open provider model, you can conveniently build providers to work with the OSC to display social network data (such as friends, profiles, activities, and relationship information) in Office.</span></span>
  
<span data-ttu-id="afa54-108">除公共社交网络站点，您还可以使用 OSC 提供程序扩展性构建提供程序的业务线应用程序或内部企业网站并将其服务集成到 Office。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="afa54-108">Apart from public social network sites, you can also use OSC provider extensibility to build providers for line-of-business applications or internal corporate web sites and to integrate their services into Office.</span></span>
